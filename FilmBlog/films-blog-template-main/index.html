Three Favorites Movies?

From the superhero genera

to take you stright to the answer it would be Man of Steel, Captain America: The First Avernger, and the Tobey Maguire Spider-man. The first connection you might make, is they all wear blue and red. The connection to why these 3 movies are my favorite or why I choose these them is more then what color suit they have. The heros are united because they give a sense of insperation after you finishing watching the movie. For me Superman is hope, Captain America is courage, and Spider-man is to be greater.

Man of Steel(2013)

"Man of Tomorrow"

In the world of DC comics, Superman is nicknamed "Man of Tomorrow". He is nickanmed this because it represents the ideals and potential of humanity. And we get to learn what it all means through this movie. In the beginning Superman struggles to understand humanity in the movie, Even when he gets older and puts the cape on, the lingering of "can humanity still be trusted" clouds him. It's until he takes a leap of faith, and begins to protect and serve humanity. He begins to slowly embody hope for humanity even if they off as untrusting to him

Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

Morality & Courage

In the cinematic universe of Marvel, Captain Americaan is known more for his morality. Doing the right thing no matter the cost. However what I consider is equally as important as morality is having the courage to help push you to your ideals. in the beginning before he became the super solider we all know, a gernade is thrown in the middle of a platoon of U.S. Army trainee soldiers. While everyone ducked and cover, the pre-destined captain america jumped onto the gernade, attempting to cover the blast with his own body. The gernade ended up being a fake for trainning purposes. Though it is important to have morals, what seperates the good from the great is the courage to try.

Spider-Man (2002)

Be Greater

Out of all the Spiderman movies to choose from this is the best one for me. The reason why is because of the three triliogies, this one best represents the 'Peter Parker' of all three. Just as many challenges and villan he faces when he is spiderman. When he takes of the mask and has the same weight of challanges but as Peter Parker. It makes you wonder why do anything regarding other in the first place? It is in hopeless situations that he is in, Spiderman/Peter Parker tries to overcome his problems and utlize his' talents to leave a postive impact on the world.
